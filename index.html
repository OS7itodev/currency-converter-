Currency Converter

Tasks

1. Currency Exchange

Convert Egyptian pounds to dollars (1 dollar = 30.9 EGP).

2. User Info & Welcome

Enter name, birth year, mobile and color. Validates input, calculates age, and shows a welcome message.

3. Equal Function

Takes three integers and returns how many are equal (0, 2 or 3).

4. Sum Values

Keeps reading numbers until you enter 0 or the sum passes 50, then shows the total.

5. Heading Tags

Displays your message as headings h1 to h6 using a loop.
